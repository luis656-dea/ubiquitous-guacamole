Privacy Policy

Your data,
your device.

Everything stays local. We don't collect or sell your information.

Information We Collect

Profile Information: Career goals, experience level, industry preferences, and professional background you voluntarily provide during profile setup.

Chat Conversations: Messages and conversations with our AI career companion.

Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and time spent in the app.

Purchase Data: Information related to in-app purchases and subscription status.

Payment Information: Processed securely through Apple's App Store — we do not store payment details.

Device Data: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

App Usage: Crash reports, performance data, and feature usage analytics.

How We Use Your Information
  • Provide and improve our AI career companion services
  • Process subscription payments and manage your premium account
  • Personalise your experience and provide relevant career advice
  • Analyse app usage to improve functionality and user experience
  • Send important updates about the app and your subscription
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud
Data Storage

UserDefaults: Message count, subscription status, and timer data stored locally via iOS UserDefaults.

Conversation Memory: Chat history and summaries stored locally on your device.

Profile Data: Your career profile is stored locally on your device.

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Your data is not transmitted to our servers without your explicit consent.

Subscriptions & Payments
  • Premium subscriptions offered through Apple's In-App Purchase system
  • Prices and terms clearly displayed before purchase
  • All payments processed securely through Apple
  • Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period
  • Manage your subscription through your Apple ID account settings
Your Rights
  • View and modify your profile information at any time
  • Delete your conversation history through the app settings
  • Cancel your subscription through your Apple ID account settings
